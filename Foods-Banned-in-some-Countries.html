9 Foods That are Banned in Different Countries

In some countries of the world, some very normal foods for us are prohibited and forbidden for them, for reasons that are sometimes logical, and sometimes exaggerated and unreasonable.

Food prohibitions can be defined as rules, codified by religion or otherwise, that do not permit eating foods or food groups. In certain cases, it is thought to be the result of health considerations or other practical reasons; In other cases, it is linked to human symbolic systems.

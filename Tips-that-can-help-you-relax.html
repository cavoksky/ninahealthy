Breathing exercises are one of the most basic relaxation techniques, and they can effectively calm your stressed-out body and mind at any time and in any place. Sit or lie down in a quiet and safe location, such as your bed or the floor of your home, and place one hand on your belly. Breathe in slowly to a count of three, then out slowly to the same count. Repeat five times more or as many times as you need to feel relaxed.

When we are mentally stressed, we frequently experience physical stress as well. Releasing physical tension can aid in the relief of stress in the body and mind.
